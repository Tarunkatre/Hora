MAKE IT REAL

When it came to designing the characters, we soon noticed that the ingredients appeared somewhat lost in the black and white treatment, making them too similar to the human characters and the backgrounds. In order to address this, we made a bold

decision to create each ingredient in 3D, incorporating comic-style features as hand-drawn overlays. This approach added a sense of realism and naturalness to the appearance of each ingredient, while maintaining the signature style.
